How to use PiXL resources in your lesson

You don't need all 6 resources every lesson. Start with just the Do Now. Add more as you get comfortable.

Phase Time What to do Which resource
Do Now 5 min Project questions on board. Students answer from memory. No notes. KnowIT Questions (.docx)
Vocab 3 min Show 4-5 key terms. Students self-check: do I know these? KnowIT PPT - pick ONE slide with definitions
I Do 10 min Talk through 5-8 slides. Skip what they already know. Don't show the whole PPT. KnowIT PPT - pick 5-8 slides max
We Do 10 min Project 2-3 questions on board. Work through as a class. Pick the hard ones. GraspIT Answers - project on board
You Do 10 min Hand out worksheet. Students work independently. Self-mark from Answers. GraspIT Write On - print for students
Exit Ticket 5 min 1-2 challenge questions for stretch, or redo 3 recall questions to check it stuck. ThinkIT or re-use KnowIT Questions

A. Full Revision Lesson

Do Now > I Do > We Do > You Do > Exit. The complete structure. Best when students need reteaching before practice.

B. Practice-Heavy Lesson

Do Now > We Do > You Do. Skip I Do. Straight into questions together, then independent. Best when they know the content but need exam practice.

C. Retrieval + Reteach

Do Now > I Do > We Do > Exit. No independent practice. Focus on gaps and reteaching. Good for weaker groups or shorter lessons.

D. Independent Revision

Do Now > You Do > Exit. Minimal teacher input. Students work through Write On independently, self-mark. Good for cover lessons or practice time.

Key rule: The KnowIT PPTs are long. Do NOT try to go through the whole thing. Pick 5-8 slides that match what you're revising today. Think of it as a resource bank, not a lesson you deliver start to finish.
