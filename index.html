What is each resource?

PiXL gives each resource type a name. Here's what they actually are in plain English.

KnowIT

A PowerPoint that teaches the key facts for a topic, plus a separate Word doc with recall questions. The PPT is for you to present. The questions are for students to answer from memory.

Use for: Do Now, I Do, retrieval quizzes

GraspIT

Application questions (not just recall). Comes in 3 versions: Write On (students write answers on the sheet), Reusable (answer on separate paper, reuse the sheet), Answers (your mark scheme).

Use for: We Do, You Do, homework, revision

ThinkIT

Challenge questions for your top students (grades 7-9). PowerPoint format. Higher-order thinking, not just harder recall.

Use for: Exit ticket stretch, extension, triple science

GetIT

Simplified, scaffolded resources for students working at grades 1-2. Accessible language, basic recall. Cross-board (not Edexcel-specific but content applies).

Use for: Intervention, SEN support, catch-up

Knowledge Mat

A dense, detailed single-page summary of everything in a topic. Information-heavy. Best for teachers to check their own knowledge, or print as A3 wall displays. Not for handing to students as-is.

Use for: Planning, wall display, teacher reference

Spine Mat

A simpler one-page summary of the core "spine" of a topic. Comes in filled (reference) and blank (students complete from memory) versions. Much more student-friendly than Knowledge Mats.

Use for: Retrieval practice, vocab check, revision

Command Words DTT

Diagnose-Therapy-Test packs focused on exam command words (describe, explain, compare, evaluate, etc.). Students identify their weak command words, practise, then test themselves.

Use for: Exam technique lessons, intervention

Therapy & Testing

Ready-made PowerPoints on the topics examiners say students struggle with most. Based on examiner report analysis. Includes Edexcel-specific command word PDFs.

Use for: Targeted intervention, reteaching weak areas
The lesson structure in a nutshell:
Do Now = retrieval (KnowIT Questions or Spine Mat blank)
Vocab/Glossary = key terms (Knowledge Mat or Spine Mat filled)
I Do = teacher models (KnowIT PPT)
We Do = guided practice together (GraspIT Answers on board)
You Do = independent practice (GraspIT Write On)
Exit Ticket = check understanding (ThinkIT or quick questions)
